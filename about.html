About Us

We are a small car business located in Sarajevo. The main purpose of our business is to achieve for your car a relevant placement in the tuning world.

We can boast successful business years back, as well as large number of satisfied customers.

In addition to providing you with a group of detailing services, we also offer a group with basic services such as external and internal car washes, as well as engine ashes.

We are looking forward to seeing you, yours FINISH LINE.
